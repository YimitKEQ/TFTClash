What is TFT Clash?

TFT Clash is a free competitive platform for Teamfight Tactics players. Every Saturday, players register for a weekly clash, compete in 8-player lobbies across multiple rounds, and earn points based on their placement. Points accumulate across the season to determine the overall champion.

It is the closest thing to an official community league for TFT: proper scoring (based on the EMEA Esports Rulebook), real tiebreaker logic, season-long standings, and a permanent record of every champion.

How It Works

  • Register: Sign up with Discord, then register for the next Saturday clash. Takes 30 seconds.
  • Check In: Confirm your attendance before the start time so the host can set final lobbies.
  • Compete: Play your assigned lobbies in standard TFT. Report your placements after each game.
  • Earn Points: 1st = 8 pts, 2nd = 7 pts, down to 8th = 1 pt. Points carry over every week of the season.
  • Climb the Leaderboard: Season standings update live after every clash. Top player at season end is crowned champion.

The Points System

TFT Clash uses the official EMEA Esports Rulebook scoring: 1st place earns 8 points, 2nd earns 7, and so on down to 8th place earning 1 point. This means every game matters. Consistent top 4 finishes beat occasional wins paired with bottom placements.

Tiebreakers (applied in order): total points, then wins plus top 4s (wins count double), then placement distribution (most 1st places, then 2nd, etc.), then most recent game finish.

Frequently Asked Questions About TFT Clash

What is TFT Clash?
TFT Clash is a free competitive platform for Teamfight Tactics players. It runs weekly tournaments every Saturday, tracks seasonal standings, and maintains a Hall of Fame for past champions. Players sign up with Discord and compete in 8-player lobbies for points that accumulate across the season.
How do I join TFT Clash?
Go to tftclash.com and click Sign Up. Use your Discord account to sign in instantly. Then register for the next Saturday clash. That is it. No email verification, no waiting period.
Is TFT Clash free to play?
Yes. Competing in weekly clashes is always free. There is no entry fee, no paywall, and no pay-to-win mechanic. Pro and Host tiers exist for extra features but are not required to compete.
When is the next TFT Clash tournament?
TFT Clash tournaments run every Saturday. Check the events page at tftclash.com/events for the exact time of the next clash and to register your spot.
What regions can play in TFT Clash?
EUW, EUNE, and NA players can all compete. Link your Riot ID on your profile to display your in-game rank alongside your clash stats.
How are TFT Clash points calculated?
Points follow the official EMEA Esports Rulebook: 1st = 8 pts, 2nd = 7 pts, 3rd = 6 pts, 4th = 5 pts, 5th = 4 pts, 6th = 3 pts, 7th = 2 pts, 8th = 1 pt. Points accumulate across all clashes in the season to determine season standings.
How do I see the TFT Clash leaderboard?
The live leaderboard is available at tftclash.com/leaderboard. It shows all players ranked by cumulative season points with tiebreakers applied automatically.
Can I host my own TFT tournament on TFT Clash?
Yes. Basic hosting for a friend group is free. The Host tier at $19.99/month adds branded pages, image uploads, and unlimited custom tournament creation. See tftclash.com/pricing for details.
Does TFT Clash have a Hall of Fame?
Yes. The Hall of Fame at tftclash.com/hall-of-fame permanently records every season champion, their stats, and notable records. Champions are enshrined there forever.
Is TFT Clash affiliated with Riot Games?
No. TFT Clash is an independent community platform. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Riot Games. Teamfight Tactics is a trademark of Riot Games.
