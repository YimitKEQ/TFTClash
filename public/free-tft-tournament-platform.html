TFT Clash is the only tournament platform built specifically for Teamfight Tactics. Run weekly clashes, track season-long standings, and build a competitive community — all for free.

Why TFT Clash?

Every other tournament platform was built for 1v1 or team brackets. TFT is a free-for-all with 8 players per lobby. That creates problems that generic platforms can't solve:

  • Points-based scoring across multiple games
  • Lobby reshuffling between rounds (anti-stack seeding)
  • Season-long standings with tiebreakers
  • Player profiles with tournament history

TFT Clash handles all of this natively.

What You Get (Free)

  • Weekly Clashes — Recurring tournaments with automatic scheduling
  • Season Standings — Cumulative leaderboard across all clashes
  • Live Brackets — Real-time lobby assignments and result tracking
  • Player Profiles — Stats, achievements, career history
  • Hall of Fame — Season champions and records
  • Registration + Check-in — No spreadsheets, no Discord bots needed
  • Shareable Results — Auto-generated stats cards for social sharing
