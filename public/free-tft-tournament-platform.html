Free TFT Tournament Platform

TFT Clash is the only tournament platform built specifically for Teamfight Tactics. Run weekly clashes, track season-long standings, and build a competitive community without spending anything.

Why TFT Clash?

Every other tournament platform was built for 1v1 or team brackets. TFT is a free-for-all with 8 players per lobby. That creates unique problems that generic platforms cannot solve:

  • Points-based scoring across multiple games per session
  • Anti-stack lobby reshuffling between rounds
  • Season-long standings with proper tiebreaker logic
  • Player profiles with full tournament history and career stats

TFT Clash handles all of this natively, out of the box, for free.

Everything Included at No Cost

  • Weekly Clashes — Recurring tournaments with automatic scheduling
  • Season Standings — Cumulative leaderboard across all clashes in a season
  • Live Brackets — Real-time lobby assignments and placement tracking
  • Player Profiles — Stats, achievements, sparklines, and career history
  • Hall of Fame — Permanent record of every season champion
  • Registration and Check-in — No spreadsheets or Discord bots needed
  • Leaderboard — Ranked standings with tiebreaker sorting
  • Broadcast Channel — Push real-time updates to all registered players

Built for Competitive Integrity

TFT Clash uses the official EMEA Esports Rulebook points system (8-7-6-5-4-3-2-1) with the correct tiebreaker order. No homebrew scoring. No spreadsheets. Real competitive standards from day one.

Supported Regions

Players from EUW, EUNE, NA, and other regions can all compete in the same clash. Link your Riot ID on your profile to track your in-game rank alongside your clash stats.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is TFT Clash really free?
Yes. Competing in weekly clashes is completely free. There is no paywall, no credit card required, and no feature that limits free players from competing. Pro and Host tiers exist for extra features like profile customization and branded tournament pages.
What is TFT Clash?
TFT Clash is a free competitive Teamfight Tactics tournament platform. It runs weekly clashes with 8-64 players, tracks season-long standings on a leaderboard, and keeps a permanent Hall of Fame of past champions. Players sign up with Discord, register for a clash, and compete every Saturday.
How do I sign up for TFT Clash?
Go to tftclash.com and click Sign Up. You can join instantly using your Discord account. No email verification required. Once you are in, register for the next clash and you are good to go.
What game modes does TFT Clash support?
TFT Clash supports standard Teamfight Tactics (ranked and normal lobbies). Tournaments use the official EMEA scoring system with Swiss, round-robin, or elimination formats depending on player count.
Can I use TFT Clash for my Discord server's tournaments?
Yes. Share the registration link with your Discord members. Everyone signs in with Discord, registers for the clash, and you manage the bracket and results from the admin panel. No bots required.
