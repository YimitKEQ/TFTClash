10a - Logo Assets
Brand Marks
Official TFT Clash logo variants for digital and print use. All assets are SVG (infinitely scalable). Maintain clear space equal to the icon height on all sides.

Icon Mark - Light BG

Do

  • Use official assets from this media kit
  • Maintain minimum clear space around the mark
  • Use on solid dark or light backgrounds
  • Scale proportionally, never stretch
  • Prefer the horizontal lockup for inline use

Don't

  • Alter colors, proportions, or element spacing
  • Place on busy, patterned, or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Add effects (shadows, outlines, glows, gradients)
  • Rotate, skew, or warp any logo element
  • Use the icon mark smaller than 24px in height
