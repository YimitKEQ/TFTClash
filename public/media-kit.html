02 - Audience Profile
Who Competes on TFT Clash
Every player actively competes, submits scores, and climbs leaderboards. Zero passive lurkers. This is a high-intent gaming audience.

Demographics

Age: 18-34 (82% of players)
Gender: 78% male, 18% female, 4% other
Regions: EU West (42%), NA (28%), EUE/CIS (15%), Asia-Pacific (10%), Other (5%)
Income: 64% have disposable income for gaming peripherals and subscriptions

Spending Behavior

Avg. monthly gaming spend: $45
Top categories: Peripherals (keyboards, mice, monitors), in-game cosmetics, energy drinks, gaming chairs
Purchase influence: 73% say peer recommendations drive buying decisions

Engagement Depth

TFT Clash players are not casual browsers. Average session length is 22 minutes. Players return weekly to check standings, review match history, track achievements, and register for upcoming clashes. 68% of players have their profile page bookmarked. The platform drives 4.2 sessions per user per week on average, with peak activity on tournament days (Saturday/Sunday).

04 - Logo Assets
Brand Marks
Official TFT Clash logo variants for digital and print use. All assets are SVG (infinitely scalable). Maintain clear space equal to the icon height on all sides.
Icon Mark - Dark BG
Icon Mark - Light BG
Horizontal Lockup - Primary
Stacked Lockup
Horizontal - Light BG

Do

  • Use official assets from this media kit
  • Maintain minimum clear space around the mark
  • Use on solid dark or light backgrounds
  • Scale proportionally, never stretch
  • Prefer the horizontal lockup for inline use

Don't

  • Alter colors, proportions, or element spacing
  • Place on busy, patterned, or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Add effects (shadows, outlines, glows, gradients)
  • Rotate, skew, or warp any logo element
  • Use the icon mark smaller than 24px in height
