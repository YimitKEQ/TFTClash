02 - Logo Assets
Primary Marks

Do

  • Use official logo files from this media kit
  • Maintain minimum clear space around the logo
  • Use on dark backgrounds for best contrast
  • Scale proportionally (never stretch)
  • Use the primary gold color for text references

Don't

  • Alter the logo colors or proportions
  • Place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Add drop shadows, outlines, or effects
  • Rotate, skew, or warp the logo
  • Use the logo smaller than 24px in height

07 - Brand Voice
How We Sound
When referencing TFT Clash in co-branded content, match this tone.

We Are

  • Competitive but inclusive
  • Professional but not corporate
  • Direct and confident
  • Community-first, always
  • Data-driven, results-focused

We Are Not

  • Overly casual or memey
  • Corporate or buzzword-heavy
  • Exclusive or gatekeeping
  • Hype-driven without substance
  • Pay-to-win or paywall-first
Key Phrases
Free to compete, always Elite Competition Built for the community Grassroots esports
