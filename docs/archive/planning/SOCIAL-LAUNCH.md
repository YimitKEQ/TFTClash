**Title:** I built a free weekly tournament platform for TFT - seasonal leaderboard, career stats, all ranks welcome

Hey comp TFT. I've been working on this for a while and it's finally ready for people outside my friend group to use.

**TFT Clash** (tftclash.com) is a free tournament platform built specifically for TFT. Not a generic bracket site adapted for autobattlers - everything is designed around 8-player lobbies, points-based scoring, and multi-game sessions.

**What it does:**
- Weekly tournaments every Saturday, free to enter
- EMEA rulebook scoring (8-7-6-5-4-3-2-1)
- Seasonal leaderboard with proper tiebreakers (total pts > wins+top4 > placement distribution > recency)
- Full player profiles with career stats, match history, and achievements
- Hall of Fame for season champions
- Anti-stack lobby seeding between rounds
- Sign up with Discord in literally 2 seconds

**What it costs:**
- Competing is free, always. No entry fees, no paywall on tournaments.
- Pro ($4.99/mo) adds ad-free, extended stats, profile customization

- Host ($24.99/mo) for running your own branded tournaments with entry fee collection

**Regions:** EUW, EUNE, NA - you can link your Riot ID to show your ranked tier on your profile.

I'm not pretending this is backed by a company or a big org. I built it because every other tournament tool either doesn't understand TFT's format or requires a Discord bot and a spreadsheet to function. This handles registration, check-in, lobby assignment, results, and standings automatically.

Looking for feedback and players. If you've ever wanted a structured weekly comp without the overhead of organizing it yourself, give it a shot.

## r/TeamfightTactics

**Title:** Made a free site for weekly TFT tournaments with a seasonal leaderboard - looking for players

Hey everyone. I built a tournament platform called **TFT Clash** (tftclash.com) and it's open to everyone now.

The idea is simple: free weekly tournaments every Saturday, points based on placement (1st = 8pts down to 8th = 1pt), and a leaderboard that tracks your stats across the whole season. Think of it like a TFT league you can just sign up for.

**Quick rundown:**
- Sign up with Discord (takes 2 seconds)
- Register for the next Saturday clash
- Check in when it starts, get assigned a lobby, play your games
- Points accumulate on the seasonal leaderboard
- Full profile with your match history, win rate, placement stats

All ranks welcome, Iron to Challenger. The points system rewards consistency not just wins, so even if you're not hitting first every game you're still climbing.

It's completely free to compete. There are optional paid tiers for stuff like extended stats and custom profile banners, but nothing that affects the competition.

Supports EUW, EUNE, and NA. You can link your Riot ID to show your rank on your profile.

Would love to get more people involved. Check it out at tftclash.com and let me know what you think.

## Posting Tips

- Post on a weekday afternoon/evening (NA time) for best visibility
- Reply to every comment in the first 2 hours - Reddit rewards engagement
- If someone asks a tough question, answer honestly. "I haven't built that yet" is fine
- Don't post both subreddits on the same day - space them 2-3 days apart
- r/CompetitiveTFT first (smaller, more engaged, target audience), then r/TeamfightTactics a few days later
- Don't crosspost, write them as separate posts
