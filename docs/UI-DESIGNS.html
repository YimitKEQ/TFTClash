Compliance & Protection

Privacy Policy

At TFT Clash, we take your competitive integrity and data security seriously. This document outlines how we handle player data across our tournament platform.

01.

Information We Collect

To facilitate a high-stakes competitive environment, we require certain data points from our athletes. This ensures match fairness and prevents multi-accounting.

Identity Data

Riot ID, Summoner Name, Region, and Rank Tier metadata.

Technical Data

IP address, browser type, and anti-cheat hardware identifiers.

02.

How We Use Your Data

Data processing is restricted to the essential operations of the TFT Clash ecosystem. We do not sell player data to third-party advertisers.

  • check_circle Tournament Matching: Using rank data to ensure fair brackets and competitive seeding.
  • check_circle Integrity Checks: Analyzing match history to identify potential scripting or boosting behavior.
  • check_circle Broadcast Graphics: displaying your Summoner Name and stats on official tournament streams.
03.

Third-Party Disclosures

We may share specific data with Riot Games, Inc. as part of our developer agreement to ensure compliance with the League of Legends / Teamfight Tactics Terms of Service.

"TFT Clash is an independent organization and is not directly affiliated with Riot Games. However, all tournament results are reported to the official API for MMR adjustments where applicable."

04.

Data Retention

Account data is stored for the duration of your active membership. Tournament records and historic standings are archived indefinitely as part of the public competitive record of the Obsidian Arena, unless a "Right to be Forgotten" request is formally filed.

Have questions about your data?

Our compliance team is available for inquiries regarding GDPR and CCPA.
